Income Share Agreement

Pay after you get a job. We offer an Income Share Agreement option to help make launching your new career more accessible. You only pay when you get a job paying R144k/year or more.

  • No Downpayment

    Zaio covers your costs until you land a job making at least R144k a year. There's no downpayment.

  • Monthly payments

    When you do land a job, you'll start to make monthly payments of 17% of your monthly salary for 24 months
